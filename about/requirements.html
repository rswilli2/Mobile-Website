Requirements

To use the Meredith College Mobile Web version 1.0, you'll need two things:

  1. A web-capable mobile device, such as:
    • A smartphone (including iPhone, Android, BlackBerry, Palm WebOS)
    • An iPod Touch or iPad**
    • Featurephones and other smartphones will be added in future updates.
  2. A network connection: A web/data plan from your carrier*, or a WiFi connection if your device has WiFi.

  • To get to Meredith College Mobile: On your mobile device, launch your web browser and go to http://m.meredith.edu. Meredith College Mobile will automatically detect your device type and deliver an experience optimized for it.

    • * Important note: Meredith College Mobile is a free service. Extra data charges may apply when using any website on your mobile device depending on your service plan.

    • ** On devices without phone service (e.g., iPod Touch, WiFi-connected PDA, internet tablet), links that are meant to dial a phone number will instead prompt the user to save the number as a new contact or save to an existing contact.
